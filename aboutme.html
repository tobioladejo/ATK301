From a young age I knew that there was a vast world filled with incredible narratives that needed to be told.​ Through fashion, film, and photography, I want to create stories that bring a fresh yet nostalgic perspective on the world we live in as a means of one day creating awareness and impact within my commmunity. I am a Howard University alumnus with a Bachelor's degree in Public Relations. Immediately after, I spent more time in the community through an education nonprofit as an elementary school mentor and tutor in Chicago, IL. Currently, I'm pursuing my masters in political science at Illinois State University's Stevenson Center for community and economic development. I greatly appreciate you for checking out my website. Feel free to stay in contact!
